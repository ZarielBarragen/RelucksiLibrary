What is Relucksi?

ReLucksi is...

A card game set in a fictional universe I made up. Humans were granted magic in the form of a face pased, live battle card game by who they assumed to be a Goddess, Relucksi.
She named the card game after herself.

How do you play?

Each deck has a max of 24 cards
- You can only use a card once in battle unless the card is stated otherwise
- There will be card "Formats" (Ranged, Summoner, Trap, Etc.) and card "Types/Elements" (Fire, Water, Earth, Air, Etc.)
- Each person that participates in this "game" must wear a device on their wrist. This device holds your deck, allows you to draw cards, and it magically shuffles your deck.
This is to keep you on your toes during battles (and to stop cheating as you're not supposed to know what cards you'll be getting next).
You cannot remove the device no matter what.
- Cards are hidden around the world, but if one would like to make their own cards,
they can ask the higher being and she will appoint them as a "Cardsmith," someone who creates cards to sell.
- Since cards in this universe works like spells/magic, there will be no "spell" types
- This game isn't turn based, all attacks are done in real time
- Every player has a different amount of overall health points. Lowest possible being 1000, Highest being 1,000,000 and the average being 4000.
